Intro

As a dynamic and analytical Data Analyst / Scientist, I have consistently leveraged data-driven insights to optimize business strategies and drive growth. My strong quantitative skills, strategic thinking abilities, and proficiency in data manipulation have allowed me to deliver actionable recommendations that align with organizational goals. With expertise in R, Microsoft Excel, SQL, Python, and a suite of data visualization tools like Tableau and Power BI, I bring the technical acumen required to extract meaningful insights from complex datasets, contributing significantly to decision-making processes. My experience spans industries, including education, manufacturing, and financial services, where I have been involved in predictive modeling, market research, and performance optimization.

With a solid academic background in Mathematics and several professional roles, I am equipped with a diverse skill set that includes data analysis, machine learning, and statistical modeling. I have hands-on experience with cloud services such as AWS (S3, EC2, Athena, Glue, and Quicksight) and Azure (Blob Storage, SQL Database, Data Factory, and Synapse Analytics), which I utilize for scalable data storage, analysis, and integration solutions. I have demonstrated leadership in managing large-scale projects, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and deploying statistical techniques to improve business outcomes. My continuous learning mindset is reflected in my certifications in Data Analytics, Machine Learning, and Project Management, and I am eager to contribute my expertise to a forward-thinking team, optimizing strategies through data-driven insights.
